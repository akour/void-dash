About the Game

Void Dash is a fast-paced arcade game that tests your reflexes and timing. Tap the screen to jump, avoid incoming barriers, and survive for as long as possible. What starts as a simple challenge quickly becomes an intense test of skill as obstacles speed up, patterns become more dangerous, and each new zone pushes your reactions to the limit.

Planetary Zones

  • 🌍 Earth Orbit – Gentle entry zone with turquoise theme.
  • 🔴 Mars Dust – Orange-red atmosphere and faster obstacles.
  • 🧊 Europa Ice – Icy blue visuals with new obstacle patterns.
  • 🌫️ Titan Fog – Foggy purple zone with challenging combinations.
  • ⚫️ The Void – Darkest zone with unpredictable patterns.

Features

  • Simple one‑tap controls
  • Endless arcade gameplay
  • Five unique space zones
  • Progressive difficulty
  • High score tracking
  • Offline play – no internet required
  • No ads or in‑app purchases
